Privacy Policy for Find A Letter

Effective Date: 13.11.2024

We, the developers of the Find A Letter game, respect users' right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data, including any data shared with Google Play Games Services, and how we use third-party advertising services in our game. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Data Collection

Find A Letter collects minimal user data, primarily for Google Play Games Services (such as login and leaderboard features) and third-party advertising services:

  • Google Play Games Services: When signing in with a Google account to use achievements and leaderboards, Google may collect information associated with your account, such as your unique user ID and in-game achievements. We do not have access to this personal information directly.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob, which may collect non-personal information about your device (such as device model, operating system type, and advertising identifier) to show contextually relevant ads.

2. Data Use

The data collected is used only as follows:

  • Google Play Games Services: To enable features such as achievements, leaderboards, and sign-in with Google. Any data associated with these services is managed by Google under their Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising: Data collected by Google AdMob is used to display relevant ads within the game and is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the game. Ads may be contextual and are not personalized if the game targets children, in compliance with Google Play's “Families” policy. More information on data collection by AdMob is available at the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not directly share user data. However, Google Play Games Services and Google AdMob may collect data as described above. This data is managed under Google's Privacy Policy.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to control Google Play Games sign-in and ad preferences through their Google account settings. You may also manage ads by adjusting your device settings, such as limiting the use of the advertising identifier.

6. Data Security

We do not store personal data directly, but we prioritize secure interactions with third-party services. Google is responsible for securing data associated with Google Play Games Services and Google AdMob.

7. Children's Privacy

We comply with Google Play's “Families” policy and laws for protecting children’s data, such as COPPA. We do not intentionally collect data from children under 13, and ads are configured to meet these requirements.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page, with the effective date noted. We encourage you to review this page regularly for any changes.
